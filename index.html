FAQ

Where do the funds go?

Student donations support clean and safe water projects through vetted local partners. Field updates are shared with our campus community as projects are completed.

How do I redeem the food ticket?

After donating, show your confirmation email or receipt at participating campus events or tables to receive your food ticket or treat.

Is my gift tax-deductible?

Yes. You’ll receive a receipt for your records. Please consult your tax advisor regarding deductions.
